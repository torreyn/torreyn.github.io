LEGO© DIMENSIONS™ Benny Fun Pack

Description

(from LEGO site)

Help Benny from THE LEGO MOVIE save the multiverse... with a SPACESHIP!

Take your LEGO DIMENSIONS gameplay into orbit with the buildable Benny Fun Pack. Place this spaced-out character on the LEGO Toy Pad to bring him to life in the game, then activate his special abilities to unravel puzzles and defeat enemies. Beam Benny's 3-in-1 Spaceship into the game to boost your gameplay, and rebuild it into a Lasercraft and The Annihilator for upgraded in-game abilities. Includes a buildable Benny minifigure with his megaphone gun.

  • Includes a buildable Benny minifigure with his megaphone gun and 3-in-1 Spaceship, both with a detachable LEGO Toy Tag.
  • Rebuild Benny's 3-in-1 Spaceship into a Lasercraft and The Annihilator for upgraded in-game abilities.
  • Place Benny and his 3-in-1 vehicle on the LEGO Toy Pad and launch into exciting in-game missions.
  • Activate Benny's Hacking, Sonar Smash, Master Build and Target abilities to solve puzzles and battle enemies.
  • Collect your favorite LEGO DIMENSIONS characters and use them in-game together for a crazy mash-up multiverse.
  • Visit LEGO.com/DIMENSIONS for more information on this exciting LEGO building and video-gaming experience. Ask your parents' or guardians' permission before going online.
  • LEGO DIMENSIONS Starter Pack and internet connection required.
  • LEGO DIMENSIONS expansion packs unlock additional content.
  • LEGO DIMENSIONS characters and vehicles are required to interact with the LEGO DIMENSIONS video game.
  • Benny's Spaceship measures over 1" (4cm) high, 2" (7cm) long and 2" (6cm) wide (alternative 3-in-1 vehicle builds vary in size).
