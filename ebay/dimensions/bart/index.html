LEGO© DIMENSIONS™ Bart Fun Pack

Description

(from LEGO site)

Join Bart from The Simpsons on an action-packed LEGO DIMENSIONS adventure!

Add a touch of mischief to your LEGO DIMENSIONS gameplay with the buildable Bart Fun Pack. Place your favorite neighborhood kid on the LEGO Toy Pad to bring him to life in the game, then activate his special abilities to solve puzzles and battle enemy characters. Use Bart's 3-in-1 Gravity Sprinter to boost your gameplay, and rebuild it into a Street Shredder or Sky Clobberer for extra in-game vehicle abilities. Includes a buildable Bart minifigure with his slingshot.

  • Includes a buildable Bart minifigure with his slingshot and 3-in-1 Gravity Sprinter, both with a detachable LEGO Toy Tag.
  • Rebuild the 3-in-1 Gravity Sprinter into a Street Shredder or Sky Clobberer for extra in-game abilities.
  • Place Bart and his 3-in-1 vehicle on the LEGO Toy Pad and set out on exciting in-game adventures.
  • Activate Bart's special Target and Mini Access abilities to solve puzzles and battle enemies.
  • Collect your favorite LEGO DIMENSIONS characters and combine them all in a crazy mash-up multiverse.
  • Visit LEGO.com/DIMENSIONS for more information on this exciting LEGO building and video-gaming experience. Ask your parents' or guardians' permission before going online.
  • LEGO DIMENSIONS Starter Pack and internet connection required.
  • Additional LEGO DIMENSIONS expansion packs unlock other in-game worlds.
  • LEGO DIMENSIONS characters and vehicles are required to interact with the LEGO DIMENSIONS video game.
  • Gravity Sprinter measures over 1” (3cm) high, 1” (5cm) long and 1” (3cm) wide (alternative 3-in-1 vehicle builds vary in size).
