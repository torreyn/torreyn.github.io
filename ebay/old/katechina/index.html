Noritake China

Fantasia Pattern #7532

Since 1904, Noritake has been bringing beauty and quality to dinner tables around the world. Superior artistry and craftsmanship, attention to detail and uncompromising commitment to quality have made Noritake an international trademark during this past century.

Noritake’s beautiful and unique Fantasia Pattern features gold grapes over a cream back ground accented with pastel gray and tan leaves and finished with a lovely gold rim detail. Suitable for any elegant occasion from holiday parties to a romantic dinner for two.

This fine set is made from the highest quality Ivory Porcelain, specially glazed to create a rich and warm tone. Noritake Porcelain is fired at the highest temperatures and is made of the strongest ingredients, meticulously crafted to be one of the most durable ceramic dinnerware materials in the world.

This is a near-complete set of vintage Noritake China purchased in 1968 in Japan, and given as a wedding gift. Gently used through the years, only for special occasions, you’ll notice the excellent condition of all the pieces. In less than a decade this set will be considered antique, greatly increasing its value.
