Vintage Etched Crystal Stem Ware

Wheat Spray Pattern

 

This is a near-complete set of vintage Crystal stemware purchased in 1968 in Japan, and given as a wedding gift. Gently used through the years, only for special occasions, you'll notice the excellent condition of all the pieces. In less than a five years (2018) this set will be considered antique, greatly increasing its value.

This lovely elegant pattern features a single wheat spray etched on two sides of each crystal stemware piece, directly opposite each other, with the wheat sprays facing in opposite directions. Crystal Stemware set includes martini shaker and glasses, high ball glasses, pilsner glasses, champagne glasses, port glasses, cordial glasses, water glasses, sherbert glasses, oetc.
