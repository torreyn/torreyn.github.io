Description

Love this bike! Works great, in very good condition, and is very comfortable and easy to use. Has a plush cushioned adjustable seat with a high back, hand rests, a bottle holder, and a magazine/book rack. Also has a functional heart rate monitor, which is very useful for cardio training.

The best part though, is its easy to read, use, and understand digital monitor with interactive variable computerized programming (touch screen technology). You can change levels of difficulty (1-9), and biking format: rolling hills, intervals, weight loss, mountains, etc., even during usage.
