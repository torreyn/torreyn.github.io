Custom White Gold & 3 Stone Diamond Engagement Ring and Wedding Band Set

This gorgeous ring set is both stunning and unique, a one of a kind custom piece handmade and engraved by an exclusive jeweler, Schwab Jewelers, in Century City area of Los Angeles, CA. The ring set is crafted from 14k white gold and include both an engagement ring and wedding band. Both rings are hand engraved with a lovely floral theme depicting flowering vines and leaves on the top surfaces, and a classic wheat pattern engraved on the sides. This ring set is a beautiful one of a kind set, a lovely gift for any bride!

Engagement Ring - A 3 stone diamond ring, with 6 additional small diamonds. It features one round brilliant cut diamond weighing .55 carats at the center surrounded by two smaller round brilliant cut diamond side stones with a total weight of .52 carats. There are an additional 6 round brilliant cut diamonds, 3 on each side inset into the leaf motif, with a total weight of .16 carats. These high quality diamonds are rated G-H in color and GVS-SI1 in clarity.

The accompanying wedding band is 3mm wide, and has the same intricate engraved patterns as the engagement ring. Both rings are sized to 6.5, with 2 small white gold sizing balls on the inside rim of each band, sizing them down to a size 5.5. These sizing balls are easily removed by a reputable jeweler (and the value of the 4 white gold sizing balls will easily pay for the cost of their removal).

This set was professionally appraised at $3,300 with a written appraisal for insurance purposes by Marcia Caden Jewelers of Beverly Hills, CA, in 2008, who bought out Schwab Jewelers when the owner Randy Schwab retired in 2008. In June 2012 I obtained a verbal appraisal from Marla at Jewelry Appraisal Professionals in Encinitas, CA, (see the Yelp rating). With the increase in gold valuation, since 2008, Marla valued this ring set at between $3,500 and $4,000 Fair Market Value.
